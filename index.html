Bio

I'm a visual and brand designer who starts every project with a question: what does this actually need to communicate, and to whom?

My work spans brand identity, packaging, digital product design, editorial systems, and strategic communication. At the AWS AI Cloud Innovation Center at Arizona State University, I translated complex technical architecture into accessible visual narratives for public-sector audiences — work that required equal parts design thinking and strategic clarity.

Outside of client work, I build things from my own curiosity: Buff Curl began as a personal product gap, (un)structure began as a philosophical framework, and Stapled began as a question about why finding zines is so hard when finding books is so easy.

I have mentored at the Principled Innovation Academy hackathon at ASU — the teams I worked with went on to win. A second mentorship is upcoming. I write about design, systems, and technology on LinkedIn and Substack. Currently open to full-time roles in brand and visual design.
